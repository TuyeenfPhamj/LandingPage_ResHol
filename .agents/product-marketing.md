# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-06-29*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** DST Marketing - Media giup nha hang, khach san, resort va quan cafe trinh bay hinh anh/noi dung theo cach khach de tin, de hoi gia va de dat lich.
**What it does:** Giup chu co so dich vu dinh huong lai hinh anh thuong hieu, chon goi thiet ke phu hop va co bo noi dung de khach hang de hinh dung mon an, khong gian, phong nghi, su kien va trai nghiem dich vu. Trong landing page hien tai, trong tam la tu van mien phi va dan khach vao form lien he.
**Product category:** Dich vu thiet ke/truyen thong cho nha hang - khach san.
**Product type:** Professional service.
**Business model:** Tu van mien phi ban dau, sau do de xuat goi thiet ke/phat trien noi dung theo nhu cau.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Nha hang, khach san, resort, quan cafe, dia diem to chuc su kien.
**Decision-makers:** Chu nha hang, chu khach san, chu resort, chu quan cafe, nha dau tu/quan ly dia diem.
**Primary use case:** Can nang cap hinh anh, an pham va thong diep de thu hut khach dat ban, dat phong, to chuc su kien hoac tham khao dich vu.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Chon duoc goi thiet ke nha hang/khach san phu hop.
- Co dinh huong truyen thong trong thoi gian ngan.
- Lam ro diem manh cua khong gian, mon an, phong nghi va dich vu.
**Use cases:**
- Nha hang can an pham mon an, set menu, uu dai dat ban, feedback.
- Khach san/resort can hinh anh phong nghi, sanh, khong gian va noi dung dat phong.
- Dia diem su kien can bo nhan dien chien dich va visual de chao ban dich vu.

| Chu khach san/resort | Ty le dat phong, cam nhan ve khong gian | Hinh anh phong nghi/khu dich vu chua thuyet phuc | Dinh huong hinh anh va noi dung de khach thay gia tri luu tru |

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Co san pham/dich vu that nhung cach thiet ke va truyen thong chua lam khach tin, thich va hanh dong.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Tu lam noi dung thuong thieu chuyen mon ve bo cuc, thong diep va hanh trinh dat ban/dat phong.
- Thue don vi chung chung de ra san pham dep nhung chua dung nganh nha hang - khach san.
- So chi phi cao nhung chat luong khong nhu ky vong.
**What it costs them:** Mat co hoi dat ban/dat phong, hinh anh thuong hieu kem cao cap, kho thuyet phuc khach moi.
**Emotional tension:** Lo lang ve chi phi, chat luong thue ngoai va viec khong biet bat dau tu dau.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Tap trung vao nha hang, khach san va dich vu luu tru.
- Co san pham thuc te trong cac du an nhu Grand View Palace Ha Long Hotel, Nha hang Ho Co Tien, Nha hang Thien Anh.
- Tu van theo nhu cau truoc khi de xuat goi.
**How we do it differently:** Bat dau bang viec hieu mo hinh dich vu, khach hang muc tieu va muc tieu kinh doanh, sau do de xuat huong hinh anh/noi dung.
**Why that's better:** Khach hang khong phai mua mot goi chung chung; ho co dinh huong nhanh va ro truoc khi quyet dinh dau tu.
**Why customers choose us:** Co san pham thuc te, hieu nganh dich vu, phong cach chuyen gia va cao cap.

- "Thiet ke chua dep."
